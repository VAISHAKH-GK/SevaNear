# SevaNear 

SevaNear is a mobile/web application that helps patients and bystanders discover free services near hospitals in Kozhikode ( Focused on Govt. Medical College Kozhikode at first )

## Key Features

- Free food
- Free medicines
- Dialysis
- Chemo
- Room & Shelters

## Service Details 

Service will include following details.

- Type of Service
- Timings
- Eligibility / documentes required
- Contact

## Impact

- Helps patients save money
- Reduces stress for families
- Ensures free services reach the right people
- Encourages community participation & transparency
